# US Visit Visa (B1/B2) Bank Statement Requirements - Bangladesh 🇧🇩🇺🇸

This repository serves as a quick-reference guide for Bangladeshi applicants applying for the US B1/B2 (Visit) Visa in 2026. 

## 📌 Key Financial Requirements
* **No Minimum Balance:** There is no official "fixed" amount; your balance must be logical based on your trip duration.
* **6-Month History:** Consistency in your bank statement is more important than a sudden large deposit.
* **Income Alignment:** Your transactions must match the income declared in your DS-160 form.

## 🚫 Common Red Flags
1. Sudden "Lump Sum" deposits right before the interview.
2. Irregular transaction history.
3. Financial records that do not match your professional profile.
